About

Women in Technology (WiT) is an annual event organised by PESU South Campus IEEE Student Branch to inspire, engage and advance women in engineering. The event provides professional women in technology, whether in industry, academia, or government a stage to tell their stories of success. This year, we are privileged to host six speakers who will be sharing their journeys on the platform. It aims to discuss how women can have a larger and crucial role in creating success stories in business and technology.
